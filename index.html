About Myself

I am an undergraduate of University of Colombo School of Computing reading for B.Sc. (Hons) in Information Systems. I am a person who seeks solutions for questions and to understand the inner workings of the world. The society, the nature, the way and the motivations of the people are what amazes me. I like to read philosophy and discuss philosophy with my colleagues. I am a free thinker who believes that the freedom of thinking is much more important than the freedom of speech. I am focused on research and development regarding Information Technology. Some of my interests are Computer Networks, Linux Virtual Machines configurations, Web Development, Mathematics and Machine Learning. Watching movies, listening to music, reading and blogging are some of my hobbies. I believe life does not give second chances and rather it gives brand new chances...
